Welcome to Gino's Italian Restaurant

Gino's Italian Restaurant is famous for its high quality food and excellent service in the heart of Cork City. Visit us today and try our wide selection of traditional foods and drinks, some of the best available in the area. Our chefs use fresh ingredients sourced locally,combining them with some of the best recipes to produce great Italian dishes. Every day, we buy our fresh ingredients at the English market. We have been serving food to the people of Cork for thirty years. Our restaurant offers great cuisines and you are welcome to come in and discover it for yourself.
