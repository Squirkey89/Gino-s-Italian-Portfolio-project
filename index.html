Welcome to Gino's Italian Restaurant


Gino's Italian Restaurant is famous for its high quality food and excellent service in the heart of Cork City.


Visit us today and try our wide selection of traditional foods and drinks, some of the best available in the area.


All of the food we make every day comes from the English market. We have been serving food to the people of Cork for thirty years.


Our chefs use fresh ingredients sourced locally,combining them with some of the best recipes to produce great Italian dishes.


Our restaurant offers great cuisines and you are welcome to come in and discover it for yourself.
